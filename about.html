Our Business Model

We connect the right technicians with the right projects, streamlining the repair process for both clients and service providers. Our NET-30 ACH payment terms ensure reliable and timely payments.

Clients submit their service requests, and we match them with certified technicians in our network. Technicians benefit from steady work opportunities aligned with their skills and schedules. This collaborative model creates a win-win environment for everyone.
